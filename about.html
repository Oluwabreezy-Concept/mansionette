Our Story

south_east

Our founder, AMB. ISRAEL started Mansionette because he wanted to make it easy for anyone to do what he does: make money by investing in real estate. He wanted to make sure that those he served alongside during his time in the national youth corp service would have the same opportunity to build wealth as those with financial degrees and real estate licenses. This vision extended to helping soccer moms, college students, long-haul truckers, freelancing nomads, firefighters, savvy seniors, and everyone in between save toward what matters most to them from the comfort of their phone. Investing in real estate is a great option for anyone looking to build long-term wealth that can stand up to risk and market volatility. The Mansionette team is united around our core philosophy: invest, and let compound interest do the rest.
